# Orbit-Privacy-Policy
Privacy policy for Orbit Mobile App

Effective date: 6 July 2026

Orbit Sales ("the App") is developed and operated by **ESSA GARMENTS PRIVATE LIMITED**
("we", "us", "our") for use by our field sales representatives to record visits,
orders, attendance, and related work activity. This policy explains what
information the App collects, why, and how it is handled.

The App is an internal business tool. It is not intended for use by the general
public and is not directed at children. Access is restricted to individuals
authorized by ESSA GARMENTS PRIVATE LIMITED.

## Information We Collect

| Category | What we collect | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Account information | Work email address | Used to sign in via a one-time passcode (OTP) sent by email. We do not use passwords for the mobile app. |
| Location data | Precise GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude), reverse-geocoded into a human-readable address | Captured when recording a field visit and when checking in/out of daily attendance, to confirm the location of field work. |
| Camera & photos | Photos taken through the App | Visit evidence photos, attendance check-in/check-out selfies, expense bill images, and optional photos attached to improvement suggestions. The App does not browse or access your general photo library except where a screen explicitly offers a gallery-upload option (e.g. attendance or suggestions). |
| Device information | A device identifier, device model name, and platform (Android/iOS) | Associated with your login session so we can show and, if needed, revoke active sessions per device (for account security). |
| Work activity data | Visits, orders, expenses, leave requests, attendance records, and suggestions you submit | Core business records created by using the App as intended. |

## How We Use Information

- To operate core App features: visit logging, order taking, attendance, expense claims, leave requests, and suggestions.
- To let managers (regional sales managers, sales managers, administrators) review and approve records submitted by their team.
- To secure your account, including detecting and revoking sessions on lost or unauthorized devices.
- To maintain accurate business and HR records.

We do not use your data for advertising, and the App contains no ads or ad SDKs.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for
marketing purposes. Information is only accessible to:

- Authorized personnel within ESSA GARMENTS PRIVATE LIMITED (your reporting managers and system administrators), as needed for business operations.
- Service providers strictly necessary to operate the App (e.g. our hosting provider and email delivery service used to send OTP codes), bound to use the data only to provide that service.

## Data Retention

Work activity records (visits, orders, attendance, expenses, leave requests)
are retained for as long as required for business, HR, and compliance
purposes. Location and photo data attached to a record is retained alongside
that record. Login session/device data is retained until the session is
revoked or expires.

## Data Security

Access to the App requires authentication (OTP login) and is restricted by
role. Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). Session tokens are stored
securely on-device and are revoked immediately if a device is reported lost,
an account is deactivated, or mobile access is locked by an administrator.

## Your Choices

- Location and camera access are requested only when needed for a specific action (e.g. recording a visit or checking in) and can be managed through your device's system permission settings, though declining them will limit the App's core functionality.
- Employees may contact their administrator to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data, subject to legitimate business record-keeping requirements.

## Children's Privacy

The App is intended solely for adult employees and authorized personnel of
ESSA GARMENTS PRIVATE LIMITED. It is not directed at, and we do not knowingly
collect information from, children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or
applicable law. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective
date at the top of this page.
